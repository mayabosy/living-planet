About Us

Welcome to Living Planet 2024! We are a charity organization dedicated to improving air quality within the UK. Pollution poses serious health risks, and we aim to raise awareness and provide solutions to combat this pressing issue.

Our mission is to empower individuals to make informed choices that positively impact the air we breathe. Through our campaign for cleaner air, we strive to educate the public about how their travel habits, fuel usage, and daily activities affect air quality.

At Living Planet, we are committed to sustainability and have set a goal to become climate positive by 2030. This means actively reducing our carbon footprint and contributing to environmental restoration efforts.

Our Services

  • Real-time weather updates: Access current weather conditions, forecasts data through our platform.
  • Air quality reports: Get detailed information on air quality indices to stay informed about the environment's health.
  • Route planning: Plan your route to the Living Planet HQ using Google Maps. Calculate distance, estimated travel duration, and view directions. Switch between different modes of transportation including walking, driving, and public transport.
